GET TO KNOW US

Sorai is the world's leading music network, connecting an ever-growing global audience to high quality music content for more than five years. Sorai offers fans worldwide a vast array of premium content to choose from, showcasing official music content alongside a constantly developing lineup of live performances and innovative original programming. From top superstars to rising new talents, Sorai brings incomparable cross-promotional support to artists across the musical spectrum, at every stage of their careers.
